Accounts FAQ


Q: How do I get an account?


A: Just send an email to thedumbrobloxplayer@gmail.com and make the subject "Account Request".

Make sure to include a profile picture, name, bio, and a secret code (If you want to edit later.). A guest book entry is optional.



Q: Can I get multiple Guest Book entries?


A: No. One per user.



Q: What are the guidelines for Guest Book entries?


A: Just, no scamming, no inappropriate text, no discriminatory words/phrases, profanity IS allowed, just no

harrasment of it towards another user, keep it as a joke. and obviously, we do NOT talk about the Drake and

Kendrick Lamar drama. (Or any controversial topics at all.), no politics, no images, emojis must be in the

TDICode (Custom Emoji) range. and no self promotion.



Q: Can I delete/edit my Guest Book entries?


A: You're going to have to put the secret code given in the initial "Account Request" email in a new email called "Guest Book Edit/Removal"



Q: Can I delete/edit my User acount?


A: You're going to have to put the secret code given in the initial "Account Request" email in a new email called "Account Edit/Removal"
